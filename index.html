Unlicensed Driving Fines by Age Group

An interactive visualisation exploring age and location-based patterns in unlicensed driving fines across Australia.

Home | Data Analysis

Target Audiences

🧑‍⚖️ Government Policy Makers

Need to compare jurisdictions and identify risk age groups for better policy planning. See the grouped bar chart.

🎓 Youth Safety Advocates

Focus on educating young drivers. Pie chart highlights youth’s proportion of fines, ideal for awareness campaigns.

📊 Researchers & Analysts

Need detailed insight into age vs region trends. Heatmaps and stacked charts help in deep comparative analysis.

Key Insights

  • Age 26–39 consistently receives the highest number of fines in all jurisdictions.
  • In states like NSW and VIC, fines for 17–25 are also significantly high, indicating a need for youth education programs.

Data Source

Data sourced from the Bureau of Infrastructure and Transport Research Economics (BITRE), 2023.
