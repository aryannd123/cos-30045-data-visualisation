Detailed analysis of unlicensed driving fines across age groups and jurisdictions in Australia.

Exploratory Observations

  • NSW recorded the highest number of unlicensed fines overall, particularly in the 26–39 age group.
  • VIC had more fines in the younger 17–25 group compared to other states.
  • Some jurisdictions (e.g., ACT) show fewer fines, which may reflect population size or enforcement differences.

Recommendations

Based on the data trends, the following actions are suggested:

  • Enhance education programs targeted at drivers aged 17–39.
  • Implement state-specific campaigns in VIC and NSW.
  • Use mobile enforcement strategies in high-fine zones.
